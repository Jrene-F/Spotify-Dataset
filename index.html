In the dropdown list, the top 200 most popular songs of 2020 and 2021 are listed. Choose a song and push the botton to select it. Find a yellow sphere in the graph which represents the selected song. Examine its fundamental characteristics and push the play botton to listen the song.

Song characteristics:
  • Danceability: It describes how suitable a track is for dancing based on a combination of musical elements including tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity.
    A value of 0.0 is least danceable and 1.0 is most danceable.
  • Valence: A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 describing the musical positiveness conveyed by a track. Tracks with high valence sound more positive (e.g. happy, cheerful, euphoric), while tracks with low valence sound more negative (e.g. sad, depressed, angry).
  • Acousticness: A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 of whether the track is acoustic.
  • Energy: Is a measure from 0.0 to 1.0 and represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity. Typically, energetic tracks feel fast, loud, and noisy.

    • Sphere characteristics:
  • Colour: Based on energy, it goes from purple fading to green, starting from 0.0 to 1.0.
  • Dimension: Based on streams, that is how many times the song has been listen, using normalized values.
  • Position: Based on valence, acousticness and danceability values as x-axis, it goes from (0.0 , 0.0, 0.0 ) to (1.0 , 1.0 , 1.0).
